AI Research Paper Analyzer

Ever wish you could just chat with a research paper? That’s what this tool is for. With AI Research Paper Analyzer, you upload any academic PDF, ask questions in plain English, and the system digs through the paper for you. Suddenly, even the densest research starts making sense.

What can you do with it?

- Upload research papers (PDFs)
- Ask anything about the document
- Get quick, accurate answers pulled straight from the paper
- Understand even complex research in less time
- Enjoy a simple, interactive interface

How does it work?

Just drop in your PDF. The app pulls out the text, runs it through AI, and builds a version you can search and question. When you ask something, it finds the right parts of the paper and gives you answers right away.

Tech Stack

- Python
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- AI APIs / LLMs
- PDF processing
- Vector search and document retrieval

Getting Started

1. Clone the repo:  
git clone https://github.com/arushkumarme/AI-Research-Paper-Analyzer.git

2. Jump into the folder:  
cd AI-Research-Paper-Analyzer

3. Install what you need:  
pip install -r requirements.txt

4. Fire it up:  
python app.py

How to Use

- Upload your PDF research paper
- Ask questions—anything you’re curious about
- The AI reads the paper and gives you answers based on what’s actually written

Sample questions:

- What’s the main contribution of this paper?
- How did the researchers do their study?
- Can you sum up the key findings?
